Biryani

Description

Biryani is a delicious South Asian rice dish made with aromatic spices and tender meat.

It is a festive dish that is perfect for special occasions and family gatherings!

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Biryani spices
  • Yogurt
  • Ghee
  • Fresh mint

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions in ghee until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes, yogurt and spices.
  5. Cook until chicken is tender.
  6. Layer rice and chicken mixture in a pot.
  7. Cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh mint and serve!
