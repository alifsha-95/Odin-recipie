Lasagna

Description

Lasagana is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and melted cheese .

It is a hearty and delicious meal perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarellad cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown ground beef with garlic in olive oil
  4. Add tomato sauce to beef and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce , and cheese in a baking dish
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used
  7. Bake for 45 minutes untill cheese is golden brown.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving
