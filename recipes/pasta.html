Pasta

Description

Pasta is a classic Italian dish that is loved all around the world.

It is quick and easy to make and can be served with many different sauces!

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Drain pasta and add to sauce.
  7. Serve with parmesan cheese on top.
